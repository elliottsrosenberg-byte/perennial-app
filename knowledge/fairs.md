# The design fair calendar

category: fairs

## Spring — New York (May)

- **Sight Unseen Offsite** — May, NYC — emerging and established designers, strong press presence.
- **ICFF (International Contemporary Furniture Fair)** — May, NYC — commercial and contract furniture.
- **Collective Design** — May, NYC — design collectibles; gallery representation is often required to exhibit.

## Milan (April)

- **Salone del Mobile / Eurosalone** — April, Milan — the global design industry epicenter. The single most important week on the international calendar for visibility.

## Miami and Basel (December / June)

- **Design Miami** — December in Miami, June in Basel — blue-chip collectible design, dominated by major galleries. Exhibiting is typically gallery-mediated.

## Paris and London (October)

- **PAD** — Paris (October) and London (October) — high-end collectible design.

## Other

- **Object & Thing** — biennial, NYC — curated design objects and editions.
- **1stDibs Introspective** — online platform, expanding.

## How to use the calendar

Booth costs range from $3,000 to $15,000+ and applications for the strongest fairs often close months ahead — plan applications and cash flow around the calendar, not around the event date. Time new-work launches and press outreach to fair openings when possible.
