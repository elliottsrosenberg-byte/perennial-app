## Round 2 — Onboarding, Cross-module, Empty states, Button audit

### 10. Onboarding flow (`components/layout/OnboardingModal.tsx`)

Three-step welcome modal shown to every first-time user (detected by missing `profiles.studio_name` + no `perennial-onboarded` in localStorage):

- **Step 1 — Welcome**: Shows what Perennial is (Projects, Contacts, Finance, Ash) with icons and 1-line descriptions. "Set up my studio" or "Skip setup".
- **Step 2 — Studio**: Studio/practice name input + practice type multi-select (same options as Settings). "Continue" disabled until name is entered.
- **Step 3 — Meet Ash**: Explains what Ash can do with 5 example prompts. "Open Ash & get started" saves the profile, fires `profile-updated` event so Sidebar updates immediately, then opens Ash with a personalized first message.

Mounted in `app/(app)/layout.tsx` so it appears on any first page load.

### 11. ProjectDetailPanel — Finance cross-module section

When a project has logged time or invoices, a **Finance** section now appears in the left sidebar between the details and the workspace navigation:
- Shows "Time logged" in hours
- Shows "Billable" amount if it's a client project with a rate
- Shows "Invoiced" count + total for non-draft invoices linked to this project
- "View in Finance →" link
- Data fetched from `time_entries` and `invoices` on panel open

### 12. ContactDetailPanel — Finance cross-module section

When a contact has invoices sent to them, a **Finance** section appears in the left sidebar:
- Shows invoice count and total amount invoiced
- "View invoices →" link to Finance tab
- Data fetched from `invoices` on panel open

### 13. Empty states — Finance tabs

| Tab | Empty state |
|-----|-------------|

All three distinguish "truly empty" from "filtered to no results" — only show the educational copy when zero records exist total.
